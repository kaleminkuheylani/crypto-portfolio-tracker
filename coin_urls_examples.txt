## How the Sitemap Works

The sitemap dynamically generates URLs for:
- Top 200 cryptocurrencies by market cap
- Each coin gets two pages:
  1. A detail page using the coin's ID: /piyasa/[coin-id]
  2. A chart page using the coin's symbol: /grafik/[coin-symbol]

Priority is assigned based on market position:
- Top 10 coins: Priority 0.9
- Coins 11-50: Priority 0.8
- Other coins: Priority 0.7
- Chart pages: 0.2 less priority than detail pages

Update frequency:
- Coin pages: hourly (due to changing prices)
- Blog pages: weekly
- Static pages: daily
